The Best Movies According to Hanna

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

If we think about our goals and dreams, they all have to start off as ideas in our minds.

The Island

The movie has a pro-life message against human cloning and against harvesting human tissue for selfish purposes,
and is a slam-bang action thriller with jaw-dropping effects.

Well I say that this is one of the best movies that I have ever seen and I mean that 100%.
